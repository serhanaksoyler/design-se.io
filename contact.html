Serhan Aksöyler, themselves

Serhan Aksöyler is a graphic and experience designer based in Toronto who believes that human kind is insufficient to deliver words besides that words are also being insufficient from the beginning due to their lack of ability to represent multiple perspectives.

As they feel perfectionist in some cases, they don’t deny words are key to communication between individuals and tries to stand along with a strong language use.

They are mostly focused on creating brand identities and user experiences. Their goal is to support minimal design for a more accessible future supported by the society itself.
